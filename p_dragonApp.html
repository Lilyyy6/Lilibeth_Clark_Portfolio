MagiQuest Silver Dragon

2018

Personal

MagiQuest is an interactive game located in many Great Wolf Lodges. Set up for the game is installed all around the hotel. I made this app while at a Great Wolf Lodge to help me and my siblings complete the Silver Dragon Quest, which was very challenging. There was a part during the quest where you have to watch the screen for a series of glowing crystals to flash. You then had to repeat the color pattern on your own screen by clicking the crystals in the correct order and then casting at the dragon before it got back to the scene. These color combinations got up to seven colors, which was very difficult to remember and cast in time.

This app has five buttons, one for each color of the crystals. When you click a button, the color is added to the box list at the top of the screen. Using this, one player can watch the screen and record the colors as they flash, and then read the combination out to the casting player at their pace, allowing the casting phase to go quicker. This allowed us to complete this quest.
